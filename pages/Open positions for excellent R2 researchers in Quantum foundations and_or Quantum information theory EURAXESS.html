Offer Description

Mathematical Institute of the Slovak Academy of Sciences is looking for 1-2 excellent researchers at the stage R2 for research in the field of Quantum foundations and/or Quantum information theory. We focus on the applications of various aspects of the theory of operator algebras, category theory, algebraic and order structures, as well as convex analysis, to these rapidly developing fields.

Our institute has a long tradition as one of the leaders in the  development of Quantum Structures,  and more recently  also contributed to the research in Quantum information theory. Accepted applicants should make an outstanding contribution to these fields, publish papers in first-class journals, actively attend conferences and seminars, and find partners for collaboration in and ouside of the institute.

The positions are to be funded within the Recovery and Resilience project for Slovakia, offering fellowships for 12-24 months.  Applicants are expected to write a research proposal that will be evaluated by the Research and Innovation Authority of the Government of the Slovak Republic. The proposed project must end before the end of June 2026.

Additional Information

Benefits
  • about 2300 Eur monthly salary (netto), including social and health insurance
  • additional budget for research expenses up to 1300 Eur per month (to be specified in the proposal)
  • flexible working hours
Selection process

The selection process has two stages. First, until the deadline on September 4, 2023, the applicant should send 

  • a CV including a list of publications and citations
  • a proof of obtaining a PhD (or equivalent)
  • a concise but reasonable description of the project objectives and methods.

The application will be evaluated by a committee of the institute.

At the second stage, the selected applicant(s) will elaborate the full project proposal in cooperation with a supervisor in our institute.  The proposal must be finished by Sept. 21. The final proposal will be evaluated by the Research and Innovation Authority of the Government of the Slovak Republic. The position will be granted if the project is successful.

Additional comments

For  more information, please send an email to jenca@mat.savba.sk
